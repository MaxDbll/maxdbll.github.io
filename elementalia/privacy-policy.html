Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Elementalia, a mobile game available on iOS and Android. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

Elementalia does not collect any personal user data. The game does not require a login or account creation, and no identifiable information is collected when you play.

Use of Analytics and Crashlytics

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to enhance the user experience and identify technical issues. Firebase Analytics collects anonymized information about app usage, such as frequency of use and actions taken within the game. Firebase Crashlytics collects data on app crashes and errors to help us fix issues promptly.

These services do not collect any personal information, and the data collected is used solely for performance tracking and app improvement purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties, as no personal data is collected by the app.

Security

We are committed to protecting the data collected by analytics and crash reporting services. The information collected is anonymized and secured in accordance with Firebase's security policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about any changes.
